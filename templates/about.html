Project Methodology

Random Forest Regressor

The core of InsurProtect is an optimized Random Forest Regressor using 500 decision trees to ensure stable and accurate predictions.

Training and Testing

The model was developed using an 80-20 split methodology, with 20 percent of data kept aside for rigorous testing.

Feature Engineering

We enhanced the data by calculating BMI and adding interaction terms to understand deeper medical risks.

90.2% Forecasting Accuracy

Reliable performance across all medical profiles

Industry Transparency

Insurance pricing is often unclear. This project aims to show how specific health factors directly impact premium costs, helping users understand their financial commitments.

Predictive Health Planning

Beyond just a calculator, this tool helps in long term planning. Users can see how managing chronic conditions or lifestyle choices influences their insurance outlook.
